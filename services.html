ISO Accreditation

The International Standards Organization is a regulatory independent Organization that maps out standards for company processes in terms of Quality, Safety, and Environment of the management System. Goldvent audits and issues certification in compliance with such standards.

ISO System Certification enhances the reputation of your Organization service or product. ISO Certification provides your Organization credibility and also gives confidence to the potential clients that you will keep your promises. The main objective of ISO certification is to promote the Continual improvement of your Organization management systems.

QA/QC Inspection and Audit

Audit steps include: review the appropriate regulations; review selected contracts and subcontracts and identify clauses requiring quality assurance systems or programs; review the applicable requirements; obtain copies of recent internal or external audit reports related to quality assurance; identify any significant system weaknesses noted therein and evaluate the status of corrective items; etc.

This document provides audit steps that include procedures for the review of materials receiving, subcontractor quality control, metrology and calibration, and quality assurance data.
